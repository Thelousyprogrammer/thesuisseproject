Chart Formulas

Cumulative Progress vs Goal C_actual(t) = Σ hours_i C_ideal(t) = workdays_elapsed × DAILY_TARGET_HOURS C_proj(t+k) = C_actual(t) + (pace × k_workdays)
  • t: current date index in timeline
  • k: projected workdays ahead
  • hours_i: hours on record i
  • pace: selected/estimated daily pace (hours/day)
Session Delta (vs 8h) Δ_i = hours_i - 8 ΣΔ_t = Σ (hours_i - 8)
  • Δ_i: daily over/under target for day i
  • ΣΔ_t: cumulative delta up to date t
  • 8: baseline target used by the chart
Day-of-Week Velocity (Radar) avg_d = (Σ hours_on_d) / count_d
  • d: weekday bucket (Mon..Sat)
  • hours_on_d: hours logged on weekday d
  • count_d: number of entries for weekday d
Performance Matrix (Correlation) Output = (Tasks / Hours) correlated with Identity Score
  • Identity Score (Alignment)
  • Hourly Output (Tasks/hr)
  • Bubble Size: Relative Session Duration
Weekly Delta Momentum (Candlestick) delta_i = hours_i - 8 open_w = first(delta_i), close_w = last(delta_i) high_w = max(delta_i), low_w = min(delta_i)
  • w: selected week bucket
  • open_w / close_w: first/last delta of week w
  • high_w / low_w: highest/lowest delta in week w
Identity Alignment identity_avg_w = (Σ identityScore_i) / count_w target = 4.0
  • identityScore_i: identity score for entry i
  • count_w: entries in week w with valid identity score
  • target: reference guide line (4.0)
Energy Zones Elite: hours ≥ 8 and personalHours ≥ 1 Overdrive: (hours + personalHours) > 9 Solid: hours ≥ 8 | Survival: hours ≥ 6 | Recovery: otherwise
  • hours: OJT hours for a day
  • personalHours: personal project hours for a day
  • Each record maps to one zone based on rule priority order
