DTR FAQ

Common questions about using the Custom Daily Time Record tracker.

What is this DTR for?

This DTR is used to log your daily OJT hours, reflection, accomplishments, and telemetry so you can track progress and performance trends over time.

How do I add a daily entry?

Go to the main page, set the date and hours worked, complete your notes, then click Save Day. Your record is saved locally in your browser.

Can I edit or delete entries?

Yes. You can use the viewer/edit tools to update a specific record, delete the last entry, or clear all records from local storage.

What is Telemetry View?

Telemetry View provides visual analytics such as pace, forecast completion, delta trends, and wellness indicators based on your DTR logs.

Where is my data stored?

Data is stored in your browser (localStorage and IndexedDB for images). It is not automatically synced to a server unless you add one.

Why are some icons or style changes not updating immediately?

Mobile browsers cache aggressively. Refresh the page, clear site data/cache, or reopen the tab after theme/icon updates.
